# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — ChimeraShield

## Primary ICP: "The Accountable SMB Owner"

### Demographics
- **Company size:** 5–50 employees
- **Revenue:** $500K–$10M/year
- **Industries (highest pain):**
  1. Healthcare (HIPAA compliance anxiety)
  2. Legal / Law firms (client confidentiality)
  3. Financial services / Accounting firms
  4. E-commerce (payment data, chargebacks)
  5. SaaS startups (IP protection, investor requirements)

### Psychographics
- Knows they "should" care about security but doesn't know where to start
- Has been hit by phishing or ransomware at least once, or knows someone who has
- Wants a simple, non-technical dashboard — not a SIEM
- Makes decisions fast (no 6-month procurement cycle)
- Will pay monthly for peace of mind

### The Trigger Events (When They Buy)
- Just got phished or had an employee click a malicious link
- About to raise a funding round (investors ask about security posture)
- New compliance requirement (HIPAA, SOC 2, PCI-DSS audit coming)
- Just hired employee #10 and feels "too big to ignore security now"
- Competitor got breached and it made the news

### What They Are NOT
- Enterprise companies with a CISO
- Government or defense contractors (too regulated)
- Individuals / consumers (wrong price point)

## Secondary ICP: "The Overworked IT Generalist"
- Single IT person managing 20–100 users
- Responsible for "everything tech" including security
- Wants automated monitoring so they stop getting blamed when things go wrong
- Budget authority: up to ~$500/month without approval

## Watering Holes (Where to Find Them)
- LinkedIn (search: "IT Manager", "Operations Manager", SMB size filters)
- Reddit: r/msp, r/sysadmin, r/smallbusiness
- Facebook Groups: Small Business Owners groups
- Industry associations: AICPA (accountants), ABA (lawyers), MGMA (medical practices)
- Referral partners: bookkeepers, accountants, IT MSPs
