# Problem Statement — ChimeraShield

## The Problem

Small and medium businesses (SMBs) are the #1 target for cyberattacks — yet they have no access to enterprise-grade AI security tools.

### The Numbers
- 43% of all cyberattacks target SMBs (Verizon DBIR)
- 60% of SMBs that suffer a major breach close within 6 months
- Enterprise AI security tools cost $50,000–$500,000/year — far out of reach for SMBs
- 73% of security professionals say AI-powered threats are already hitting organizations (2026)

### The Core Pain
An SMB owner with 5–50 employees has:
- No dedicated security team
- No budget for enterprise tools
- No visibility into whether their systems are being probed or compromised
- No way to know if their employees are being targeted by AI-generated phishing

### The Status Quo (What They Do Today)
- Rely on basic antivirus (Malwarebytes, Windows Defender)
- Ignore security until something breaks
- Hire an IT generalist who "also handles security"
- Pay for a reactive incident response after the breach

### Why Now?
1. AI-powered attacks have lowered the cost of attacking SMBs to near zero
2. The tools to defend against AI attacks now exist (LLMs, agentic AI)
3. No one has productized this for the SMB market at an accessible price point

## The Opportunity
Build the "$99/month AI security co-pilot" for SMBs — giving them capabilities that cost enterprises $500K, for the price of a Netflix subscription.
